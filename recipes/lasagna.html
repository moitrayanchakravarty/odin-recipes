The recipe to make Lasagna

Description :

Making a lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps :

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
